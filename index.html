Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cleaning services

How often should I schedule professional cleaning?

The frequency depends on your specific needs. For regular maintenance, we recommend bi-weekly or monthly cleaning. For deep cleaning services, quarterly appointments are often sufficient.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, our professional team brings all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. We use eco-friendly products that are effective and safe for your family and pets.

How long does a typical cleaning service take?

Service duration varies based on the size of your home and the type of cleaning. A standard cleaning for a two-bedroom apartment typically takes 2-3 hours, while deep cleaning may take 4-6 hours.

Is your service insured?

Yes, GleamUpp is fully insured. Our insurance covers any potential damages that might occur during the cleaning process, giving you peace of mind.
