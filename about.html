Our Story

We don't just clean homes.
We create breathing room.

Since 2020, GleamUpp has been redefining what it means to come home. We believe a clean space isn't a luxury—it's the foundation for a happy, healthy life.

Driven by Care, Not Just Chemicals

GleamUpp started with a simple realization: people are busier than ever, and home maintenance shouldn't be another source of stress. We set out to build a service that combines 5-star professional standards with the personal touch of a neighbor you can trust.

Every member of our team is hand-picked, background-checked, and trained to treat your home with the same respect they would their own. We use eco-friendly products because we care about the planet, but more importantly, because we care about the air your family breathes.
